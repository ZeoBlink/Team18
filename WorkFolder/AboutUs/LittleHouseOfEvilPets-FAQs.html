FAQs

What kind of pets do you sell?

Our selection includes the usual cats and dogs. However, once in a while we would have an exotic pet for sale too. These include, but not limited to, bunnies, bats, and dragons.

What is a companion pet?

We train our pets to be the perfect companion for all our customers. They will be there to listen to you as your recite your evilish-plans of world domination, of how you will crush your rivals to oblivion, and to comfort you as your plans are crushed by the hero who saves the day. They will always be there for you, one way or another.

Can I sell my pet to House of Evil Pets?

If it is evil enough, yes. We gauge this by whether it is willing to eat its owner or not.

What is your return policy?

Please refer to our Returns Page.

I believe you sold me a defective pet. My pet is acting up, he will not listen to me and attacks anyone he sees including myself.

That is your problem and probably your personality. He is doing exactly what he was trained to do which is to reflect upon his owner’s personality traits.

I know your store sells evil pets, but are the pets really evil?

Yes. They are evil. They are evil beyond comparison.

Is it possible to convert a pet I bought from you from evil to good?

I don’t believe our pets are suitable for you and this would be a violation of our company policy. If we find out that you are trying to convert one of our pets to good, we will be expected to blow you to oblivion. (Please see section ‘Intention to convert our pets to good’ of our Company Policy page).
